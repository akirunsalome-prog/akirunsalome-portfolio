About Me

I don't run ads.
I build acquisition systems.

"Most media buyers optimise for the metrics that look good in a report. I optimise for the ones that actually matter to the business."

Most paid ads underperform not because the targeting is wrong, but because nobody has thought clearly about what success actually means for that specific business. A 4% CTR means nothing if the WhatsApp funnel is broken. A low CPL means nothing if the product margins can't sustain it.

I start with the business model, then build the campaign architecture backwards from it. That's why I built a custom Google Sheets tracker for a client whose Meta Ads couldn't measure WhatsApp conversions — and why I diagnosed a location targeting failure before it burned through budget.

I manage live Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns across Search, Display, and Meta — including click-to-WhatsApp, traffic, and engagement objectives. Full campaign lifecycle: strategy, setup, targeting, optimisation, reporting. I work independently and I communicate clearly. No noise, just numbers that move.
